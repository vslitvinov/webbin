DCA bots are trading bots that allow users to automatically buy and sell cryptocurrencies at recurring intervals over a set period.

DCA trading is for investors who want to reduce the impact of volatility and gradually accumulate or lower their positions. This mechanism allows users to average the price of their tokens by purchasing an established amount over time.

For example, if you enter the market with one investment, there is a risk that you have just bought an asset at a high price. The value will decrease after a while, resulting in a loss-making position.

However, if you invest by dividing the amount into parts, you can buy assets at different times and prices. It leads to a more favorable average cost, meaning that you will significantly reduce the risks of local price changes.
