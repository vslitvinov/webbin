DCA Bot
Averaging and Loss Protection Strategy

Get Started WATCH VIDEO

Everything you need

DCA bots are trading bots that allow users to automatically buy and sell cryptocurrencies at recurring intervals over a set period.

DCA trading is for investors who want to reduce the impact of volatility and gradually accumulate or lower their positions. This mechanism allows users to average the price of their tokens by purchasing an established amount over time.

For example, if you enter the market with one investment, there is a risk that you have just bought an asset at a high price. The value will decrease after a while, resulting in a loss-making position.

However, if you invest by dividing the amount into parts, you can buy assets at different times and prices. It leads to a more favorable average cost, meaning that you will significantly reduce the risks of local price changes.

Frequently asked questions

How our DCA Bot work ?

The DCA bot automatically buys and sells the cryptocurrency at recurring intervals for a specified period.

If you enter the market, investing all your capital at once, you risk buying at a high price and losing money when the price falls. Investing in installments allows you to reduce the risks of local price movements by allocating investments over time.

How this algorythm work ?

Simply saying, instead of a risky one-time investment, which may go into minus if you do not guess the market rate, we propose dividing this amount into parts and investing each with maximum benefit. As a result, instead of buying 100 shares for 100 dollars, on average, we will buy them for you 80-85 in installments. And when the price rises, you will sell them for a higher price smoothly.

Can i use multiple coin pairs ?

Of course, the bot can work with different pairs of currencies or groups by switching between them depending on the situation and user settings.
